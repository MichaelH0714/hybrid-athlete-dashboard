Protocols & Recovery

Athleticism is built in the recovery. Do not skip these steps.

⚡

Warm-Up (Mandatory)

Perform before every session. Critical for explosiveness.

  • 1
    5 Min Cardio Row, Bike, or Elliptical.
  • 2
    Dynamic Activation Leg Swings, Arm Circles, Hip Circles.
  • 3
    Movement Prep Bodyweight Squats, Lunges, Piston Hops.
💤

Recovery Fuel

You only grow when you rest.

Sleep

Target 7-9 hours. This is when hormonal regulation happens.

Protein

1.6g - 2.2g per kg of body weight daily.

Hydration

Water + Electrolytes essential for power output.

📝

Coach's Notes

Power Execution: Rest 90-120s. Every rep must have MAXIMAL intent, even if the weight moves slow.

Strength Execution: Follow the RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion). If form breaks, drop weight.

Pain vs. Soreness: Joint pain = Stop/Swap. Muscle soreness = Go.
